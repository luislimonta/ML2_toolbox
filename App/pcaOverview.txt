We can find an overview of the loadings of the principal components.
From this we can try to interpret the meaning of the components.
    
In this case PC1 seems to represent the wine Type. It has low values for withe Wines and high values for red wines.
PC2 represents the density with low values for heavy, sugary wines and low values for light wines with higher alcohol amount.
PC3 seems to represent Acidity or the Ph-Value
PC4 seems might represent quality of the wine: In terms of sulphates.
